Event Overview

SMCC-CCIS Week 2026 is an annual campus event that highlights student projects, programming skills, innovation, cybersecurity awareness, and collaboration among IT professionals.

Join us for a week of learning, networking, and fun & excitement as we celebrate technology in education. ✓

Event Schedule ✓

Here is the simplified schedule of activities:

  • Day 1: Opening Program and Keynote Talks
  • Day 2: Coding Hackathon
  • Day 3: Robotics and IoT Exhibition
  • Day 4: Cybersecurity Awareness Session
  • Day 5: Project Showcase and Awarding Ceremony

Location

All events will be held at the SMCC-CCIS Building, Main Campus. Room assignments per activity will be posted here once finalized.
